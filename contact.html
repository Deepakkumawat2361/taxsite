Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete my tax return?

Most tax returns are completed within 48 hours of submission. Complex returns may take up to 5 business days. You'll receive regular updates on the progress of your return.

What if I need to make changes to my tax return?

You can request changes to your tax return before it's filed with HMRC at no additional cost. After filing, we can help you submit amendments if necessary.

Are your accountants qualified?

Yes, all our accountants are fully qualified and accredited by major UK accounting bodies. They have at least 5 years of experience and undergo regular quality audits.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. All our accountants are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

What if HMRC has questions about my return?

Your assigned accountant will handle any HMRC queries on your behalf. This support is included in our service at no extra cost.

Can I speak directly to my accountant?

Yes, once you're matched with an accountant, you can communicate with them directly through our platform or by email throughout the process.
